Description of Target Audience

The target audience for this project is the general public who live in the South Eastern part of Idaho. Whether you are an avid outdoorsman, planning an outing, or simply trying to plan your day, you will find this page useful.

5 Scenarios

Scenario 1:

A group of friends is going to go fishing, they check the weather to be able to take the necessary things to be successful.

Scenario 2:

Erica, a parent to three young children, checks the daily weather forcase before picking out her children's clothes for the day. She needs to know if rain is expected and what the high and low temperatures are forcasted to be.

Scenario 3:

The Skywalker family is planning a surprise birthday party for their young twin children, Luke and Leia. To make sure that the party is as great as possible, they need to know the weather forcasted for the next few days. If it is going to rain, they should plan to bring an umbrella. If it is going to snow, they should plan to bring a snowshoe, and if it is going to be a storm, they should plan to bring a boat.

Scenario 4:

The Payton Child Youth Services is hosting a soccer tournament for the local intramural teams. They need to know the expected temperatures and weather forcasted for the weekend, so they can send out an email to all the parents. They will keep track of the forcasted weather in case they need to reschedule the tournament.

Scenario 5:

A local high school teacher is looking at taking some time off of from their class. They need to know the weather forcasted for the next few days, so they can plan accordingly.
